# AI Policy Communicator

**Workflow:** Transform internal AI governance policies into role specific plain-language summaries.

**User:** HR teams, department managers, and compliance officers at mid-size enterprises.

**Input:** Raw AI policy text (copy-pasted or uploaded as a string).

**Output:** Three tailored summaries - one for frontline employees, one for managers, one for executives — each with a different tone, length, and focus.

**Why automate this:** Most companies write AI policies that sit in a PDF no one reads. This tool bridges the gap between legal/compliance language and day-to-day understanding — a real and growing need as AI governance regulations tighten globally.
